How SocioVault works

01

Supporters send optional one-time or recurring deposits to the shared vault. Every dollar is timestamped and logged in the public ledger.

02

Anyone seeking help records a short video, adds proof, and submits their story. Moderators verify identity and documentation before publishing.

Frequently asked questions

Is SocioVault a bank?

SocioVault operates as a community treasury with audited accounts. We safeguard money like a bank but distribute it democratically.

Can I withdraw my contribution?

Yes. There‚Äôs a 48-hour reversal window before each weekly vote. After funds are allocated you can choose to pause future deposits.

How do you prevent fraud?

Every request is identity-checked, receipts are collected post-payout, and suspicious activity is reviewed by a rotating community council.

What fees does SocioVault collect?

We charge 3% to cover payment processing, moderation, and audits. Fee usage is disclosed in the ledger each month.
